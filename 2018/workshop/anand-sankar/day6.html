Day Six

Day Six was a direct continuation of Day Five, zero sleep but the adrenaline kept me going, at about 05:00 AM, Anand Parikh and some of the mentors asked me and the rest of the guys who were still at the lab if we wanted to go trekking to the nearby mountain (actually it was a really big and steep hill but mountain seems apt and sounds cooler) and we rounded up some guys and called some other people who were sleeping like Madhav, another mentor and Abhishek(from Team F), and we all went trekking.

Me and my friend Anuvind(from Team D) were leading and we sorta raced up the hill, got hella exhausted by the time we got all the way to the top, but we got there JUST in time for sunrise, and it was beautiful. We called it TrekTop 2018 (pun intended)



We rested for a while, soaking in the beautiful landscape around us and then we began our descent; We spotted theses really cool looking chameleons but our phones ran out of battery battery before we could take a picture, and as we reached the base, there was this peculiar rock formation which Anuvind, me and Madhav spotted, probably some metamorphic rock, but it was shiny and reflected light in rainbows, it was really cool, and we took some samples as souvenirs because they were so pretty looking.

I went back to the lab and by then, the impeller had finished printing and then I assembled it and went to sleep for a few hours.

I returned back to the lab afternoon and by then everyone had been building and working on their presentations.
