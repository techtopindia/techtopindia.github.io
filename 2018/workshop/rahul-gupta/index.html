  • Add flowchart with a little description of each step.
  • Write about Technology and Design you used or created. (add a link if you have)
  • Few challenges which your team faced and how all together with the team it was solved.
  • Briefly describe the role of your team members and inputs with mentor support.
  • End up with the final conclusion of target achieved of solving a problem statement.

Exposure Visit to Gautham’s Farm – In a next day session we went to a farm for a exposure visit and I observed a many things their i.e. about cows, its food and milk, shepherd and its work to take caring of cow and maintaining all the things, farming etc. He has to do lots of hard work to get milk from a cow and not just a milk they also use the cow dung in various kind of uses like as a fertilizer, bio fuel or bio product etc. and they used also a urine of cow in a medical purpose. They give all the cow dung and urine to the medical person to use it and what other thing I observed i.e.

That there are the two species of cows available i.e. 1. Jersey and 2. Indian.

There are many differences in these two species.

The jersey cows give a milk count around 35-40 litres milk in a one day of compare to the Indian cow which gives only 5-10 litres a day.

The jersey cow has a short lifespan than that of Indian cow as the injection is given to jersey to increase the quantity of milk.

The jersey cow also has a different shape like of its shoulder, horns and neck than Indian cow.

The jersey cows have less protein and calcium in it’s milk and other one Indian cows having all these quality in just same quantity of milk and it’s milk is also so strong and so good for a born child.

The jersey cow’s milk has a low price rate compare to Indian cow.

There is arrangement for the cow food they are using the process hydroponic to grow the cow food in any climatic condition. they use water for it and produce a corn type food for cow. They also are growing crop there and some of the papaya’s trees and they are also growing super naferal grass which has a quality that when we cut that grass it will automatically grow again upto 3 years.

 

I also noticed about that what problems they are facing while doing that great work i.e.

When we are feeding seeds to grow a papayas tree than every time rabbit came and ate that seeds so they are failed to grow the papayas. Anyway the farm is surrounded by walls and all the cows are protected.[e][f]

Day 4 –

Arumuga Group Cotton Mill

Today they planned for a visit so we started at 9 am to go for an exposure visit in a cotton mill at chatrapatti so we all are the participants and meteors are in same bus did lot of fun with together and at around 10 am we reached to the cotton mill, so after reaching there we have a seminar and they tell about the history of that mill, how they works and all the detail about the mill.

There were some of the observation that I noticed there I.e.

They started their mill on 1994.

They were making yarn after processing of raw material of cotton.

They are collecting raw material of cotton from all over villages of India.

The raw material having lot of impurities.

They are storing a raw material of cotton in a store room in a bundle form by applying compression on it and each bundle having a size of around 50 kg.

They are making the yarn size of 1.875 kg.

They can store that cotton for maximum 2 years.

The cotton contains lot of impurities.

They are processing the cotton to remove its impurities and with the help of machines and sensors.

Problems –

Workers are not wearing masks there in that dusty situations.

It can be injurious to their health.

 

Sri Venkteshvara Vilas sweet shop

Some of the observation I noticed there i.e.

They were making palkova sweet by mixing milk and sugar together in a ratio of like that they are adding 2kg of sugar in 15 kg of milk and after heating they got 6 kg of palkova.

They are exporting that sweet to all over world.

They are packing that sweet in a small box to sell it.

Problems –

Blind School

We visited to the blind office at kalasalingam university such a great initiative started by them. There was some of the physically challenged peoples were there which are learning computer software there.  

Observation –

They (disable persons) are really so hardworking and they are curious to learn about computer software.

They are good observer and follower as they are working on computer which as NVDA software to giving them instruction.

They are independent peoples.

They have lot of problems i.e.

NVDA software instruction voice is like robot voice so it is not easy to understand.

They are having limited job opportunities.

They are facing a problem in recognizing of bus stop and place when they were travelling.

They are having problem with glass doors.

The navigating new places is so difficult for them.
